Starting from concerns about the increasingly critical condition of agricultural land, due to the use of chemical fertilizers and the lack of utilization of organic waste which is still an environmental problem, especially in the areas of North Sumatra and Aceh, PT Siba Agro Mandiri is here with a solution.

PT Siba Agro Mandiri is here as a company engaged in the distribution and marketing of Liquid Organic Biofertilizer Biokonversi located and Granular Organic Biofertilizer Biokonversi in Medan, North Sumatra.

As a company that is always developing and innovating, we always want to develop markets and sales channels throughout North Sumatra and Aceh.

We will always be ready to serve customers of Liquid Organic Biofertilizer Biokonversi & Granular Organic Biofertilizer Biokonversi
